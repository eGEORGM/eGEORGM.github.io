SHORT PROGRAM

Each university organises 10-14 days short program once a year and sends 5-10 undergraduate or postgraduate students. In addition to take mini-classes, hands-on classes, and visit companies, they will have culture experience, deepen their cross-cultural understanding, and acquire communication skills through various exchange activities.

Scholarship by the Japan Student Services Organisation (JASSO) is available (Eligible for those who meet the academic and financial requirements)

Support for travel expenses and accomodation is available
